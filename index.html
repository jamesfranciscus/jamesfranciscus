Experience

SIGNAL 88 SECURITY (CONTRACTED SECURITY COMPANY)
MARCH 2016 TO PRESENT

I started at the Octorara School District as an armed security officer. I made patrols by foot and automobile of all schools. I acted as a deterrent to crime and enforced school district rules and policies. July 2016 I was promoted to an operations manager. I am responsible for the overall operations of the Brandywine Hospital, Phoenixville Hospital and Jenner’s Ponds accounts. This includes quality control, hiring, firing, and training security officers.


EAST PIKELAND TOWNSHIP
JUNE 2002 UNTIL MAY 2016

I was the Chief of Police and responsible for the overall operations of the Police Department. This included budgeting, planning, hiring, disciplinary actions, interacting with elected officials, and citizen’s groups, problem solving, scheduling and investigations. I retired from this position May 2016.



EDUCATION

I am a graduate of the Coatesville Area School District. I also graduated from the Pennsylvania State Police Academy and the F.B.I. National Academy. I have also attended many management and criminal justice training classes during my career.


COMMUNICATION

During my career, I, have had to communicate both verbally and in writing to elected officials, neighborhood association groups, District Attorney’s Office, news media, subordinates, citizens in the community, along with people under stressful situations.
I feel that I have kept my superiors well informed and have been an effective communicator with others I have encountered.


LEADERSHIP

I have held many leadership positions during my career and I feel that I have been an effective leader. I have been a Chief of Police in two Chester County communities, President of the Chester County Police Chief’s Association. I have always lead by setting a good example, setting the bar high for subordinates, helping to develop employees to do a better job and learn all they could about their chosen career. I have always tried to be a coach and cheerleader to the people I served with.
